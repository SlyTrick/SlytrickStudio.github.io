Dielivery

A spooky and cute game where you can make people happy in the afterlife and become the employee of the month

Meet the main character

Get in the "bones" of Grimmy, the worker for a delivery company called Dielivery.

Frenetic and quick

In Dielivery, the goal is to create packages containing all of the offerings the dead recieve on Earth and send them to their reciever. In order to do that, you must hone your parkour skills and find out the best routes to get the offerings you need

Become the employee of the month

Explore all the different maps and warehouses of Dielivery and try to get the best score by delivering as many packages as you can within the time given, to ultimately become the employee of the month

Appear in the game!

Make your appearance in Dielivery in the shape of one of the dead that make requests to Grimmy, simply interact with this tweet and comment the way you think you would die. Your name, profile picture, and the death reason will have a chance to be one of Grimmy's orders
